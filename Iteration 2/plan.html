What is the plan?

Clearing our waste from their habitats.

What can we do to help them?

Our website aims to provide useful knowledge to audience on what are the harmful behaviors to seals. Besides, we provide a platform where the rubbish collector can exchange the rubbish to coffee or something else from nearest store who is willing to offer free treatment to the collectors. We will also provide the rubbish collector a short questionnaire for rubbish data collection.

We can protect their environment by volunteering for activities that can clear the plastics from the beaches and reporting injured animals on the beaches. We can do activities that can reduce their risks of getting involved with plastics. Volunteering to help the marine animals is a service that one can do for the betterment of nature.

Beach wastes are one of the main sources of marine pollution. The purpose of this plan is to clear the beaches of waste so that the marine animals are safe. We would like to stop the problem at the source.
